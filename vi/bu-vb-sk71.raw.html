Bhikkhu Vibhanga - Sekhiya 71: Not to teach the dhamma to someone who is walking ahead of you unless he is ill.

  • Translated from the Pali by Ebo Croffie (coming sooner or later).

    • … in Anāthapiṇḍika’s monastery. Now at that time the group of six monks, going behind, taught dhamma to (someone) going in front … " …

    ‘I will not teach dhamma, going behind, to (someone) going in front (and) who is, not ill,’ is a training to be observed."

    Dhamma should not be taught, going behind … if he is the first wrong-doer.

1 Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū pacchato gacchantā purato gacchantassa dhammaṃ desenti … pe …  .

2 "Na pacchato gacchanto purato gacchantassa agilānassa dhammaṃ desessāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā"ti. (71:216)

3 Na pacchato gacchantena purato gacchantassa agilānassa dhammo desetabbo. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca pacchato gacchanto purato gacchantassa agilānassa dhammaṃ deseti, āpatti dukkaṭassa.

4 Anāpatti—  asañcicca … pe …   ādikammikassāti.

5 Ekādasamasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
