Bhikkhu Vibhanga - Sekhiya 13: To speak in a low voice when sitting in inhabited areas.

  • A Saddhamma Study Group translation from the Pāḷi text (coming sooner or later).

1 tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū uccāsaddaṃ mahāsaddaṃ karontā antaraghare gacchanti … pe …  .

2 "appasaddo antaraghare gamissāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā"ti. (13:158)

3 appasaddena antaraghare gantabbaṃ. yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca uccāsaddaṃ mahāsaddaṃ karonto antaraghare gacchati, āpatti dukkaṭassa.

4 anāpatti—  asañcicca, assatiyā, ajānantassa, gilānassa, āpadāsu, ummattakassa, ādikammikassāti.

5 tatiyasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
