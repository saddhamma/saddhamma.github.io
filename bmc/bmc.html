Buddhist Monastic Code

  • Translated from the Pali by Thannissaro bhikkhu. For more copyright information please see: here.

BMC1 is primarily concerned with the Pāṭimokkha rules while BMC2 is primarily concerned with the Khandaka rules although there is no clear demarcation between the two volumes and much of the material overlaps.

Abbreviations Used in BMC 1 and BMC2

Numbers in the references to Mv, Cv, and Parivara denote chapter, section and sub-section; in the references to DN, Iti, Khp, and MN, discourse (sutta); in the references to AN, Cp, SN, and Sn, section (saṃyutta or nipāta) and discourse; in the references to Dhp, verse.
