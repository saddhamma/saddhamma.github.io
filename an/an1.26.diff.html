1. Obsession of the Mind - 26

  • A Saddhamma Study Group translation from the Pāḷi text.

1 “I, O bhikkhus, do not see even one other dhamma which [when] thus brought into being, manifested, leads to great benefits as this, the mind. The mind, O bhikkhus, [when] brought into being, manifested, leads to great benefits.”
