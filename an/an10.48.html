10: The Book of the Tens

48. Things

  • A Saddhamma Study Group translation from the Pāḷi text.
    Get PDFs: Side-by-side | Par-by-par | Facing pages | English only | Pali only.

1 thus (or yes) me the heard (learning): At one time the Auspicious One At Savatthi abides jetavane of/for anāthapiṇḍika a's park. at that place the Auspicious One addressed bhikkhu bhikkhu s. “Bhante.” those (by/with/for you) bhikkhu s replied to the Auspicious One. the Auspicious One said this:

2 ten these O bhikkhu s liable to from one gone forth to abhiṇha should be paccavekkhīti. which one? ten :

3 in (or on/at/within/herein/among) vevaṇṇiya ajjhupagato (“|”) from one gone forth to abhiṇha should be paccavekkhiti.

4 parapaṭibaddhā me jīvikā (“|”) from one gone forth to abhiṇha should be paccavekkhiti.

5 aṭṭo me ākappo to-be-done (should be acted upon) (“|”) from one gone forth to abhiṇha should be paccavekkhiti.

6 kaccinu kho me self from virtue not upavadatī (“|”) from one gone forth to abhiṇha should be paccavekkhiti.

7 kaccinu kho me having found out viṭṭū life of purity from virtue not upavadantī (“|”) from one gone forth to abhiṇha should be paccavekkhiti.

8 by which (or with or from) (the) all (s) me by which (or with or from) dear (affection/beloved) (s) by which (or with or from) pleasing (s) various bhāvo vinābhāvoti' from one gone forth to abhiṇha should be paccavekkhiti.

9 kammassakomhi kammadāyādo kammayoni kammabandhu kammapaṭisaraṇo which (in that) deed (action) I will make noble ṃ or evil what? (or who or whom?) or of/for that (or him) heir I will (or could) be (“|”) from one gone forth to abhiṇha should be paccavekkhiti.

10 talk about of/for [earth bound] devas me rattindivā vītipatantī (“|”) from one gone forth to abhiṇha should be paccavekkhiti.

11 kaccinu (indeed) I suññāgāre abhiramāmī (“|”) from one gone forth to abhiṇha should be paccavekkhiti.

12 atthinu kho me a beyond-human quality alamariyaṭāṇadassana distinction attained sohaṃ pacchime at the time of by which (or with or from) companions in/of the life of purity (s) asked not maṅkubhavissāmī (“|”) from one gone forth to abhiṇha should be paccavekkhiti.

13 these kho O bhikkhu s ten liable to from one gone forth to abhiṇha should be paccavekkhiti (“|”).

14 idamavoca the Auspicious One. pleased those (by/with/for you) the bhikkhus for the Auspicious One said they abhinanduti.

1 evaṃ me sutaṃ: ekaṃ samayaṃ bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. tatra kho bhagavā bhikkhu āmantesi bhikkhavoti. bhadanteti te bhikkhū bhagavato paccassosuṃ. bhagavā etadavoca:

2 dasa ime bhikkhave dhammā pabbajitena abhiṇhaṃ paccavekkhītabbā. katame dasa:

3 vevaṇṇiyamhi ajjhupagato'ti pabbajitena abhiṇhaṃ paccavekkhitabbaṃ.

4 parapaṭibaddhā me jīvikā'ti pabbajitena abhiṇhaṃ paccavekkhitabbaṃ.

5 aṭṭo me ākappo karaṇīyo'ti pabbajitena abhiṇhaṃ paccavekkhitabbaṃ.

6 kaccinu kho me attā sīlato na upavadatī'ti pabbajitena abhiṇhaṃ paccavekkhitabbaṃ.

7 kaccinu kho maṃ anuvicca viṭṭū sabrahmacārī sīlato na upavadantī'ti pabbajitena abhiṇhaṃ paccavekkhitabbaṃ.

8 sabbehi me piyehi manāpehi nānā bhāvo vinābhāvoti' pabbajitena abhiṇhaṃ paccavekkhitabbaṃ.

9 kammassakomhi kammadāyādo kammayoni kammabandhu kammapaṭisaraṇo yaṃ kammaṃ karissāmi kalyāṇaṃ vā pāpakaṃ vā tassa dāyādo bhavissāmī'ti pabbajitena abhiṇhaṃ paccavekkhitabbaṃ.

10 kathaṃ bhūtassa me rattindivā vītipatantī'ti pabbajitena abhiṇhaṃ paccavekkhitabbaṃ.

11 kaccinu khohaṃ suññāgāre abhiramāmī'ti pabbajitena abhiṇhaṃ paccavekkhitabbaṃ.

12 atthinu kho me uttarimanussadhammā alamariyaṭāṇadassana viseso adhigato sohaṃ pacchime kāle sabrahmacārīhi puṭṭho na maṅkubhavissāmī'ti pabbajitena abhiṇhaṃ paccavekkhitabbaṃ.

13 ime kho bhikkhave dasa dhammā pabbajitena abhiṇhaṃ paccavekkhitabbā'ti.

14 idamavoca bhagavā. attamanā te bhikkhū bhagavato bhāsitaṃ abhinandunti.
