1: The Book of the Ones

1. Obsession of the Mind - 268

  • A Saddhamma Study Group translation from the Pāḷi text.
    Get PDFs: Side-by-side | Par-by-par | Facing pages | English only | Pali only.

0 (source: sāvatthi)

1 This is impossible, O bhikkhus, there is no chance, in that an individual excelling in [noble right] view could take on any sankhara [as being] from [something] permanent. This possibility is not to be found. But this very possibility, O bhikkhus, is to be found, in that a puthujjana could take on some sankhara [as being] from [something] permanent. This possibility is to be found.

0 (sāvatthinidānaṃ:)

1 aṭṭhānametaṃ bhikkhave anavakāso, yaṃ diṭṭhisampanno puggalo kañci saṅkhāraṃ niccato upagaccheyya. netaṃ ṭhānaṃ vijjati. ṭhānañca kho etaṃ, bhikkhave, vijjati yaṃ puthujjano kañci sankhāraṃ saṅkhāraṃ niccato upagaccheyya. ṭhānametaṃ vijjatī"ti. vijjati. pts page 027
