4: The Book of the Fours

2. Fallen

  • A Saddhamma Study Group translation from the Pāḷi text.
    Get PDFs: Side-by-side | Par-by-par | Facing pages | English only | Pali only.

0 At Savathi:

1 “Those not endowed with these four dhammas, O bhikkhus, are said to have fallen from this dhamma and discipline.” Which four?

2 “Those not endowed with the noble virtue, O bhikkhus, are said to have fallen from this dhamma and discipline.”

3 “Those not endowed with the noble samadhi, O bhikkhus, are said to have fallen from this dhamma and discipline.”

4 “Those not endowed with the noble wisdom, O bhikkhus, are said to have fallen from this dhamma and discipline.”

5 “Those not endowed with the noble liberation, O bhikkhus, are said to have fallen from this dhamma and discipline.”

6 “Those not endowed with these four dhammas, O bhikkhus, are said to have fallen from this dhamma and discipline.”

7 “Those endowed with these four dhammas, O bhikkhus, are said to not have fallen from this dhamma and discipline.” Which four?

8 “Those endowed with the noble virtue, O bhikkhus, are said to not have fallen from this dhamma and discipline.”

9 “Those endowed with the noble samadhi, O bhikkhus, are said to not have fallen from this dhamma and discipline.”

10 “Those endowed with the noble wisdom, O bhikkhus, are said to not have fallen from this dhamma and discipline.”

11 “Those endowed with the noble liberation, O bhikkhus, are said to not have fallen from this dhamma and discipline.”

12 “Those endowed with these four dhammas, O bhikkhus, are said to not have fallen from this dhamma and discipline.”

“Dropped, dying, fallen,
those greedy ones come back again;
[But those who have] done what should be done, delighted in what should be delighted in;
have arrived at pleasure through pleasure.”

0 sāvatthinidānaṃ:

1 catūhi bhikkhave dhammehi asamannāgato imasmā dhammavinayā papatito'ti vuccati. katamehi catūhi?

2 ariyena bhikkhave sīlena asamannāgato imasmā dhammavinayā papatito'ti vuccati.

3 ariyena bhikkhave samādhinā asamannāgato imasmā dhammavinayā papatito'ti vuccati.

4 ariyāya bhikkhave paññāya asamannāgato imasmā dhammavinayā papatito'ti vuccati.

5 ariyāya bhikkhave vimuttiyā asamannāgato imasmā dhammavinayā papatito'ti vuccati.

6 imehi kho bhikkhave catūhi dhammehi asamannāgato imasmā dhammavinayā papatito'ti vuccati.

7 catūhi bhikkhave dhammehi samannāgato imasmā dhammavinayā appapatito'ti vuccati. katamehi catūhi?

8 ariyena bhikkhave sīlena samannāgato imasmā dhammavinayā appapatito'ti vuccati.

9 ariyena bhikkhave samādhinā samannāgato imasmā dhammavinayā appapatito'ti vuccati.

10 ariyāya bhikkhave paññāya samannāgato imasmā dhammavinayā appapatito'ti vuccati.

11 ariyāya bhikkhave vimuttiyā samannāgato imasmā dhammavinayā appapatito'ti vuccati.

12 imehi kho bhikkhave catūhi dhammehi samannāgato imasmā dhammavinayā appapatito'ti vuccatīti.

cutā patanti patitā,
giddhā ca punarāgatā;
kataṃ kiccaṃ rataṃ rammaṃ;
sukhenānvāgataṃ sukhaṃ'ti
