Dhammapada - Chapter 2: Appamadavagga - Heedfulness

  • A Saddhamma Study Group translation from the Pāḷi text.
    Get PDFs: Side-by-side | Par-by-par | Facing pages | English only | Pali only.

Vigilance is the path to the deathless, negligence is the path to death; 21
the vigilant ones do not die, the negligent ones are as [already] dead.

The wise one having known this distinction in vigilance; 22
rejoices in vigilance, delighting in the pasture of the noble ones.

The meditators who are constantly and continuously energetic; 23
those wise ones attain nibbana, the unsurpassed release from bondage.

For the mindful and strenuous one, for the one whose actions are pure, acting considerately; 24
for the discerning one living the life of Dhamma, for that vigilant one, glory ever grows.

By effort and vigilance, by discipline and [self] taming; 25
let the wise one be an island [onto oneself], which no flood can submerge.

The foolish ignorant population devote themselves to negligence; 26
but the wise protects vigilance, as his best treasure.

Do not devote yourself to negligence, do not delight in sensual pleasures and intimacy; 27
indeed the vigilant one [always] meditating, attains abundant happiness.

When a wise one expels negligence with vigilance; 28
having ascended the palace of wisdom, sorrowless in a sorrowful population;
just like the wise one standing on a mountain [top], looks down at the fools on the ground.

Vigilant among the negligent, wide-awake among the sleeping [masses]; 29
the wise ones advance, just like a swift horse having abandoned the weak horse.

It was with vigilance that Inda arrived at supremacy over the devas; 30
the [wise] praise vigilance, negligence is ever blamed.

The bhikkhu who delights in vigilance, having seen fear in negligence; 31
moves like fire, setting ablaze [all] fetters [whether] small or great.

The bhikkhu who delights in vigilance, having seen fear in negligence; 31
is incapable of deterioration, he is just in the presence of nibbana.

appamādo amatapadaṃ pamādo maccuno padaṃ 21
appamattā na mīyanti ye pamattā yathā matā.

etaṃ visesato ñatvā appamādamhi paṇḍitā 22
appamāde pamodanti ariyānaṃ gocare ratā.

te jhāyino sātatikā niccaṃ daḷhaparakkamā 23
phusanti dhīrā nibbāṇaṃ yogakkhemaṃ anuttaraṃ.

uṭṭhānavato satimato sucikammassa nisammakārino 24
saññatassa ca dhammajīvino appamattassa yaso'bhivaḍḍhati.

uṭṭhānenappamādena saññamena damena ca 25
dīpaṃ kayirātha medhāvī yaṃ ogho nābhikīrati. bjt page 32

pamādamanuyuñjanti bālā dummedhino janā 26
appamādañca medhāvi dhanaṃ seṭṭhaṃ'va rakkhati.

mā pamādamanuyuñjetha mā kāmarati santhavaṃ 27
appamatto hi jhāyanto pappoti vipulaṃ sukhaṃ.

pamādaṃ appamādena yadā nudati paṇḍito 28
paññāpāsādamāruyha asoko sokiniṃ pajaṃ
pabbataṭṭho'va bhummaṭṭhe dhīro bāle avekkhati. pts page 005

appamatto pamattesu suttesu bahujāgaro 29
abalassaṃ'va sīghasso hitvā yāti sumedhaso.

appamādena maghavā devānaṃ seṭṭhataṃ gato 30
appamādaṃ pasaṃsanti pamādo garahito sadā.

appamādarato bhikkhu pamāde bhaya dassivā 31
saṃyojanaṃ aṇuṃ thūlaṃ ḍahaṃ aggīva gacchati.

appamādarato bhikkhu pamāde bhaya dassivā 32
abhabbo parihāṇāya nibbāṇasseva santike.
