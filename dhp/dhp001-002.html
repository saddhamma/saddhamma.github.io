Dhammapada - Chapter 14 (refuge excerpt): Buddhavagga - The Buddha

  • A Saddhamma Study Group translation from the Pāḷi text.
    Get PDFs: Side-by-side | Par-by-par | Facing pages | English only | Pali only.

Mind precedes phenomena
created by mind, ruled by mind 1
If one speaks or acts
with a corrupted mind
from that suffering follows one
like the wheel, the ox's foot.

Mind precedes phenomena
created by mind, ruled by mind 2
If one speaks or acts
with a pure mind
from that pleasure follows one
like the shadow, that never leaves.

manopubbaṅgamā dhammā
manoseṭṭhā manomayā 1
manasā ce paduṭṭhena
bhāsati vā karoti vā
tato naṃ dukkhamanveti
cakkaṃ'va vahato padaṃ.

manopubbaṅgamā dhammā
manoseṭṭhā manomayā 2
manasā ce pasannena
bhāsati vā karoti vā
tato naṃ sukhamanveti
chāyā'va anapāyinī.
